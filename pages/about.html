About MC Media

MC Media believes creators deserve ownership and fair pay. In today's digital landscape, music travels across countless platforms, often losing track of its distribution and failing to credit the original creator. Our solution embeds copyright information directly into music files to create a permanent link between the work and the creator. Overall, our goal is to build tools that put power back in the hands of creators. MC Media team brings expertise in music technology and copyright management to support creators at every stage.
